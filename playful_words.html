Playful Words and Boston Public Library

Playful Words is designing tools for children to develop skills in self-expression, literacy, and dialog through natural mediums of written language, pictures, and stories. This group is working with Boston Public Library to gain an understanding of how to integrate SpeechBlocks and other Playful Words tools into existing community networks. The group is working with community partners and families to design these tools and define the role of what they call a Family Learning Coach. The exchange aims to create workshops and activities to support learning using Playful Words tools, help Family Learning Coaches learn to facilitate child and parent engagement, create tutoring programs to support early learning, and incorporate SpeechBlocks and StoryBlocks literacy tools into storytime for children and families.
