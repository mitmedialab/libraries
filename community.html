Residency Exchanges

Residency exchanges pair up teams of public librarians with Media Lab researchers to develop materials together. Meet our current residency exchange participants:

  • Playful Words and Boston Public Library
  • DataBasic.io and St Paul Public Library
  • ScratchX and Richland Library
  • Open Agriculture Initiative, Akron-Summit County Public Library, and Free Library of Philidelphia
  • Space Exploration Initiative, Charlotte Mecklenburg Library, and Cambridge Public Library

PLIX is all about building collaborations, so let’s team up!
